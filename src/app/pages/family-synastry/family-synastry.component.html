I Hold Space for Families to Harmonize

Synastry is an astrological technique that compares the dynamic interplay of celestial energies between two people. This reading is for understanding the soul contract you have with your spouse or children. You have shared past incarnations together and your souls agreed to share another lifetime together because you have unfinished business. You are meant to expand each other’s consciousness in some profound way, and synastry can illuminate these details.
